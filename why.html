Why Beetroot?

Nutritional Powerhouse

Beetroot is rich in betalains, unique antioxidants with anti-inflammatory properties shown to support heart health and detoxification[1][2]. A single serving provides:

  • ▶️ 15-20g plant-based protein (meets FSANZ high-protein claims)
  • ▶️ Natural nitrates that improve blood flow and exercise performance[3][4]
  • ▶️ 300% more antioxidants than most fruits[5]
Sources: [1] Jones 2014, [2] Mohsen et al. 2024, [3] Zielińska‐Przyjemska et al. 2009, [4] Wootton-Beard et al. 2015, [5] BBC Good Food 2024
